Privacy Policy:

Effective Date: 29.06.2023

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use [Shift] (referred to as "the App").

Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information:

When you register on the App, we collect the following personal information:

Name or nickname
Invitation code
Country

1.2 Usage Data:

When you use the App, we may collect certain usage data automatically, including your interactions with the App's features and the timestamp when you press the "start scooping" button. This timestamp is saved on our server.

Use of Information

2.1 Personal Information:

We use the personal information collected to:

Verify your identity and manage your account
Associate you with the inviter through the invitation code

2.2 Usage Data:

We use the usage data collected to:

Improve our App's features and functionality
Analyze user behavior and preferences
Provide personalized experiences
Monitor and prevent fraud or unauthorized use of the App

Information Sharing

We may share your personal information:

With our service providers who assist in delivering our services (e.g., hosting, analytics)
With your inviter who referred you to the App, linking you both within the App
When required by law or to protect our legal rights

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, update, or delete your information. To exercise these rights or if you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us using the information provided below.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on our website or through other communication methods.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at artanidos@crowdware.at.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described herein.
